💡 How It Works

This calculator models three cloud cost scenarios based on your inputs and industry-average savings benchmarks:

🟠 On-Demand Only
Pure pay-as-you-go with no commitment. Highest flexibility, highest cost. Typical savings: 10–20% vs on-prem.
🔵 Optimized Cloud (Recommended)
Reserved instances + right-sizing + spot usage. The balanced approach. Typical savings: 30–45%.
🟢 FinOps Optimized
All of the above + 3-year reserved, idle resource cleanup, storage tiering. Maximum savings: 45–60%.
